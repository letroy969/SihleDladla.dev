# Personal Portfolio Website

## Overview
This is my personal portfolio website, built using **HTML, CSS, and JavaScript**, designed to present my profile in a clear, professional, and engaging way. The site combines clean structure with a futuristic, tech-inspired aesthetic to highlight my skills, projects, and achievements.

The portfolio is intended for recruiters, hiring managers, and collaborators who want a quick but meaningful overview of my technical background and capabilities.

---

## Sections

### Hero Section
The hero section serves as the first point of contact. It introduces who I am, what I do, and my core focus areas, supported by a strong visual identity and clear call-to-action elements.

### About Section
This section provides a concise professional summary, outlining my background, interests, and career direction. It emphasizes problem-solving, continuous learning, and practical application of technology.

### Tech Stack
A dedicated section showcasing the technologies, tools, and frameworks I work with, including:
- Frontend development tools
- Programming languages
- Development environments and platforms
This gives recruiters a quick technical snapshot of my skill set.

### Certificates
This section highlights completed certifications and courses that support my technical knowledge and commitment to growth. It reflects both academic learning and industry-recognized credentials.

### Contact
The contact section provides clear ways to get in touch, making it easy for recruiters or collaborators to reach out for opportunities, discussions, or networking.

---

## Technologies Used
- **HTML5** – Semantic structure and content layout
